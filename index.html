Sweet Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

¼ cup honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp red pepper flakes
1½ tbsp olive oil
2 boneless chicken breasts

Prep

Mix ingredients. Cook chicken until browned. Add sauce and cook until thickened.

Noodle Recipe

Ingredients

1 lb steak
2 tbsp butter
1 onion
1 cup beef stock
4 cups noodles

Prep

Cook steak and onion. Add stock and simmer. Boil noodles separately and mix.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter
1¾ cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips
1 egg
½ tsp baking soda

Prep

Mix ingredients. Form dough balls. Bake at 350°F for 10–12 minutes.
