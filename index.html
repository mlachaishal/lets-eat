Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1¾ cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips

Quick Overview

  1. Brown butter
  2. Mix with sugars
  3. Add egg and vanilla
  4. Fold in flour and chips
  5. Bake at 350°F

Ultimate Meal Prep Guide

Weekly Strategy

  • Choose 3-4 recipes with overlapping ingredients
  • Set aside 2 hours on Sunday for batch cooking
  • Pre-chop veggies & marinate proteins in advance
  • Cook grains in large batches

Storage & Containers

  • Glass containers with airtight lids
  • Separate wet & dry ingredients
  • Use portion-sized containers
  • Label everything with name & date

Freezer-Friendly Tips

  • Soups, stews, and curries freeze beautifully
  • Flash-freeze meatballs, cookie dough
  • Use silicone bags for flat freezing
  • Always cool food completely before freezing

Sweet Chicken Recipe

¼ cup honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp red pepper flakes
1½ tbsp olive oil
2 chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. Whisk honey, soy, pepper flakes
  2. Heat oil, cook chicken until browned
  3. Add sauce, cook until thickened
  4. Serve hot
Tip: Marinate chicken for extra flavor!

Hearty Noodle Recipe

Ingredients

1 lb steak
2 tbsp butter
1 onion
2 cups beef stock
4 cups egg noodles
½ cup peas

Instructions

  1. Sear steak, set aside
  2. Sauté onion in butter
  3. Add steak, stock, simmer 50 min
  4. Add peas, thicken
  5. Serve over noodles
Low and slow makes steak tender!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1¾ cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Brown butter
  2. Mix with sugars
  3. Add egg, vanilla
