Sweet Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

¼ cup honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp red pepper flakes
1½ tbsp olive oil
2 chicken breasts

Instructions

  1. Whisk honey, soy, pepper flakes
  2. Heat oil, cook chicken until browned
  3. Add sauce, cook until thickened
  4. Serve hot
Tip: Marinate chicken for extra flavor!

Hearty Noodle Recipe

Ingredients

1 lb steak
2 tbsp butter
1 onion
2 cups beef stock
4 cups egg noodles
½ cup peas

Instructions

  1. Sear steak, set aside
  2. Sauté onion in butter
  3. Add steak, stock, simmer 50 min
  4. Add peas, thicken
  5. Serve over noodles
Low and slow makes steak tender!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1¾ cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Brown butter
  2. Mix with sugars
  3. Add egg, vanilla
  4. Add flour, chips
  5. Bake 10-12 min at 350°F
Chill dough for chewier cookies!
