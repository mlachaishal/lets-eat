Sweet Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

¼ cup honey
2 tbsp soy sauce
½ tsp red pepper flakes
1½ tbsp olive oil
2 boneless chicken breasts (bite-sized)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook chicken until lightly browned (about 5 minutes).
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; cook until chicken is no longer pink and sauce thickens (about 5 minutes).
  5. Serve hot over rice or steamed vegetables. Enjoy!
Tip: Marinate chicken in soy sauce for 15 minutes for extra flavor!

Hearty Noodle Recipe

Ingredients

1 lb steak (cubed)
2 tbsp butter
1 onion (diced)
2 cups beef stock
4 cups egg noodles
½ cup frozen peas
1 tbsp cornstarch slurry
2 bay leaves, 1 tsp thyme, salt & pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Sear steak in batches until browned, about 5 minutes. Transfer to plate.
  2. Melt butter and sauté onion until softened, 5-10 minutes.
  3. Add steak back with beef stock, bay leaves, thyme, salt, pepper. Bring to boil, then simmer covered 50 minutes.
  4. Stir in peas and cornstarch slurry; cook uncovered until thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove bay leaves.
  5. Boil egg noodles in salted water until al dente (about 5 minutes); drain.
  6. Serve beef mixture over noodles with extra gravy.
Chef's Note: Low and slow simmering makes steak incredibly tender. For deeper flavor, deglaze with red wine before adding stock.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter (for browning)
¾ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 egg (room temperature)
1 tsp vanilla extract
¼ tsp salt
½ tsp baking soda
1¾ cups flour
1 cup chocolate chips
Sea salt for sprinkling

Step-by-Step Brown Butter Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Brown the butter in a pot until amber and nutty (3-5 min). Remove from heat.
  3. Let brown butter cool until warm, then mix with white and brown sugar.
  4. Add egg and mix until smooth and glossy.
  5. Add vanilla and salt; add baking soda into vanilla before mixing.
  6. Add flour and chocolate chips, mix until no flour specks remain.
  7. Scoop dough balls onto prepared sheet, sprinkle with sea salt.
  8. Bake 10-12 minutes until edges golden but centers soft.
  9. Cool 8-10 minutes on baking sheet, then transfer to rack. Enjoy!
Baker's Tip: Chill dough for 30 minutes for extra chewy cookies. Brown butter adds deep caramel notes!
